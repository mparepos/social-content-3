# A-Z Services LLC — Brand Guidelines

---

## 1. Brand Overview

A-Z Services LLC presents itself as a **trustworthy, professional, and approachable** business partner. The brand should always feel like a reliable colleague — polished but never corporate, warm but never unprofessional. Every visual and verbal touchpoint should communicate: *we've got this handled.*

---

## 2. Logo

### Primary Logo
- Full wordmark: **"A-Z Services LLC"** with a small stylized hyphen-to-arrow graphic between the A and Z, symbolizing the full journey from start to finish.
- The arrow element uses the brand's Accent Gold color.

### Logo Variations
| Variation | Usage |
|---|---|
| Full color (dark background) | Website headers, presentations, dark social templates |
| Full color (light background) | Print materials, light social templates, email headers |
| Single-color navy | Letterheads, legal documents, formal correspondence |
| Icon-only (A→Z mark) | Profile avatars, favicon, app icon |

### Clear Space
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the capital "A" on all four sides of the logo.

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not place the logo on a busy photographic background without an overlay
- Do not use unapproved color combinations
- Do not recreate the logo in a different font

## 3. Color Palette

### Primary Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Navy Blue** | `#1B3A5C` | 27, 58, 92 | Primary backgrounds, headers, text |
| **Clean White** | `#FFFFFF` | 255, 255, 255 | Backgrounds, reverse text |

### Secondary Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Accent Gold** | `#C8973A` | 200, 151, 58 | CTAs, highlights, logo arrow, underlines |
| **Soft Sky** | `#EAF2FB` | 234, 242, 251 | Section backgrounds, card fills, light accents |

### Neutral Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Charcoal** | `#2D2D2D` | 45, 45, 45 | Body text on light backgrounds |
| **Mid Gray** | `#7A7A7A` | 122, 122, 122 | Subtext, captions, dividers |
| **Light Gray** | `#F4F4F4` | 244, 244, 244 | Alternate section backgrounds |

### Color Usage Rules
- **Navy Blue** is the dominant brand color and should anchor all layouts.
- **Accent Gold** should be used sparingly — for emphasis only (buttons, icons, pull quotes). Overuse dilutes its impact.
- **Soft Sky** is ideal for breaking up sections without using white.
- Never use more than 3 colors in a single composition.
- Text on Navy backgrounds must be **White** or **Accent Gold** only.
- Text on White/Light Gray backgrounds must be **Charcoal** or **Navy Blue**.

## 4. Typography

### Primary Typeface — Headings
**Font:** Playfair Display
**Weights used:** Bold (700), SemiBold (600)
**Usage:** Page titles, section headers, social post headlines, pull quotes
**Character:** Sophisticated, confident, high-trust
**Source:** Google Fonts (free)

### Secondary Typeface — Body & UI
**Font:** Inter
**Weights used:** Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600)
**Usage:** Body copy, captions, buttons, labels, data tables
**Character:** Clean, modern, highly legible at all sizes
**Source:** Google Fonts (free)

### Accent Typeface — Optional Callouts
**Font:** Lora (Italic)
**Weights used:** Italic (400i)
**Usage:** Testimonial quotes, pull quotes, taglines only
**Character:** Warm, personal, handcrafted feel
**Source:** Google Fonts (free)

### Type Scale

| Element | Font | Size | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Page Title (H1) | Playfair Display | 48–64px | Bold |
| Section Header (H2) | Playfair Display | 32–40px | SemiBold |
| Subheader (H3) | Inter | 22–26px | SemiBold |
| Body Text | Inter | 16–18px | Regular |
| Caption / Label | Inter | 12–14px | Medium |
| Testimonial Quote | Lora Italic | 18–22px | Italic |

### Typography Rules
- Headlines should always be set in **sentence case** (not ALL CAPS, not Title Case Every Word).
- Line height for body text: 1.6–1.8x the font size.
- Never use more than two typefaces in a single design.
- Minimum body text size for print: 10pt. For digital: 14px.
- Do not use decorative or script fonts outside of the approved accent typeface.

## 5. Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality
A-Z Services speaks like a **trusted business advisor** — someone who is:
- **Knowledgeable** but never condescending
- **Warm** but always professional
- **Confident** but never boastful
- **Direct** but never cold

### Platform-Specific Tone

| Platform | Tone | Style |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Professional, insightful, thought-leadership | Longer-form, data-backed, authoritative |
| Facebook | Warm, conversational, community-oriented | Shorter, emoji-friendly, story-driven |
| Email | Personal, clear, action-oriented | Concise, helpful, value-first |
| Website | Authoritative, trustworthy, benefit-focused | Clean, structured, clear CTAs |

### Voice Dos
- Lead with value and empathy
- Use "you" and "your business" frequently
- Acknowledge the challenges business owners face
- Celebrate client wins genuinely

### Voice Don'ts
- Avoid jargon and corporate-speak
- Don't over-hype or use excessive exclamation points (LinkedIn)
- Don't be preachy or lecture-y
- Avoid passive voice where possible

## 6. Imagery & Photography

### Photography Style
- **Real people, real environments:** Prefer authentic, candid imagery over staged stock photos.
- **Diversity and inclusion:** Imagery should reflect the diverse range of business owners A-Z serves.
- **Bright and professional:** Well-lit, clean environments. No dark, gritty, or overly dramatic images.
- **Business context:** Home offices, team collaboration, client meetings, professional settings.

### Graphic & Illustration Style
- Flat design with subtle depth (light shadows, soft gradients)
- Icons should be **line-style** in Navy or Gold
- Illustrations (if used) should follow a minimal, geometric style
- Infographics: Navy/White/Gold palette with Inter typeface

### Image Don'ts
- No overly staged "fake handshake" stock photography
- No images with watermarks or visible branding from competitors
- No low-resolution images (minimum 72dpi digital, 300dpi print)

### Post Dimensions (Standard)
| Platform | Feed Post | Story |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook | 1200 x 630px | 1080 x 1920px |
| LinkedIn | 1200 x 627px | N/A |
| Instagram | 1080 x 1080px | 1080 x 1920px |

### Template Structure
Every branded social post should include:
1. **Bold headline** (Playfair Display, White or Gold on Navy background)
2. **Supporting body text** (Inter Regular, White or Charcoal)
3. **Logo** (bottom right corner, small)
4. **Brand color background or photo with Navy overlay**

## 9. Brand Dos and Don'ts Summary

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Use Navy as the foundation of all designs | Use more than 3 colors in one composition |
| Lead with the client's problem, not our features | Use ALL CAPS in headlines or body text |
| Use Playfair Display for all major headlines | Use competing blue tones or conflicting color families |
| Keep compositions clean and uncluttered | Stretch, distort, or recreate the logo |
| Use Accent Gold sparingly for emphasis | Use low-quality photography or clip art |
| Speak to the business owner's real challenges | Use overly corporate or jargon-heavy language |
