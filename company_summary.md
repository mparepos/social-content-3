# A-Z Services LLC — Company Summary

## Overview

**A-Z Services LLC** is a full-service business support and outsourcing company headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia. Founded in 2015 by entrepreneur Marcus Webb, the company was built on the belief that small and mid-sized businesses deserve access to the same caliber of professional services that large corporations enjoy — without the enterprise price tag.

The name "A-Z" reflects the company's core promise: to handle every aspect of a client's back-office and operational needs, from A to Z, so business owners can focus on growth.

---

## What the Company Does

A-Z Services LLC offers a comprehensive suite of business support services across four primary divisions:

### 1. Administrative & Virtual Assistant Services
- Executive virtual assistant support (scheduling, email management, travel coordination)
- Document preparation and data entry
- Customer service and live chat support
- CRM management and database maintenance

### 2. Bookkeeping & Financial Operations
- Monthly bookkeeping and account reconciliation
- Accounts payable/receivable management
- Payroll processing
- Financial reporting and QuickBooks support

### 3. Marketing & Social Media Management
- Social media content creation and scheduling
- Email marketing campaigns (Mailchimp, Klaviyo)
- Graphic design for digital and print
- Monthly analytics reporting

### 4. HR & Staffing Support
- New hire onboarding documentation
- Employee handbook creation and updates
- Job posting and applicant screening support
- Benefits administration coordination

## Target Clients

A-Z Services LLC primarily serves:

- **Small business owners** (1–25 employees) across industries such as real estate, healthcare, legal, construction, and retail who need reliable back-office support without hiring full-time staff.
- **Solopreneurs and freelancers** who are scaling up and need their first layer of operational infrastructure.
- **Mid-sized companies** (25–100 employees) looking to outsource non-core functions to reduce overhead costs.
- **Startups** that need professional-grade operations support during their growth phase but aren't ready to build internal departments.

### Industries Served
- Real estate agencies and property management firms
- Medical and dental practices
- Law firms and legal services
- E-commerce brands
- Construction and trades companies
- Non-profit organizations

## Competitive Differentiators

- **One-stop shop:** Clients can bundle services across divisions for a seamless, integrated experience.
- **Dedicated account managers:** Each client has a single point of contact who oversees all service areas.
- **Scalable packages:** Month-to-month contracts with tiered plans that grow with the business.
- **U.S.-based team:** All staff are based in the United States, ensuring cultural alignment and time-zone availability.
- **Proven track record:** 200+ active clients, 4.9-star average rating on Google, and a 94% client retention rate.
